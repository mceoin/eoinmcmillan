Startuphouse

Shortly after arriving in San Francisco, I set up and ran a 50-person hostel for entrepreneurs called StartupHouse, the first building of its kind and still the largest by far.

When we took over the building it was a shell with no running water or electricity. StartupHouse quickly became a lesson in bootstrapping and hustle (we used cashflow to fund construction and expansion).

Ultimately, StartupHouse was a huge success. We repaid all CAPEX in the first year, maintained a 4½ star guest rating, and built a community over 400-strong.
